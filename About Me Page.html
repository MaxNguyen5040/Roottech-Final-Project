Some Foods That I like!

A quick summary of restaurants and foods I like.

Savory Meals

  • Poutine
    • Fries with Cheese and Gravy! A great mix of savory, finger food, and cheese pull!


  • Banh Canh Cua
    • Crab soup, with a lot of toppings. Super wholesome and hearty.


  • Dumplings!
    • Fried is obviously the best way to cook them, but steamed and boiled are good too.


Sweet Foods

  • Skittles
    • Little rainbow bits of goodness.


  • Coffee Crisp
    • The best candy bar ever. Think of a KitKat-Crunch combo.


  • Chocolate Mousse Cake
    • Creamy, silky, and chocolatey.


Snacks

  • Kettle Cooked Chips
    • Crunchy, flavorful, and fancy, these are a great snack.


  • Lays Wavy Garlic Roasted Sea Salt
    • Similar to kettle cooked, but pack a ton of flavor and garlic.


  • Pringles
    • Simple, crunchy, and just good.
