School

I attend Naperville North, a high school in a suburb of Chicago. I'm involved in a lot of clubs, and love the constructive learning environment.

Clubs

I'm involved with the clubs below:

  • Science Olympiad

    • Science Olympiad (Scioly for short) is a science competition based club, where you choose a few events to research and get tested on. Last competition season, my 2 events were forensics and ornithology.Forensics, like in the movies, was all about identifying chemicals through testing. Super fun, but kind of a time crunch. Ornithology is the study of birds, which was a a research heavy event. I had a lot of fun doing the raw work to compile notes.



  • DECA

    • DECA is a business club, where you try to compete against others in different formats, like a multiple choice test, or a interview style conversation. I had a ton of fun studying and improving on my verbal skills. Also, I'm the Fundraising and Philanthropy Intern at my chapter. (Soon...)




  • The Merry Tutor

    • The Merry Tutor is a student run tutoring organization! I think it's a great way to help and teach younger students.




  • STAC

    • STAC is a student led committee that gives students the opportuinities to see how school technology works, and gives them a say in the matter.
